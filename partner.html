INTEGRATION TYPES

Three ways to connect
with Zeno

🖥️

POS Integration

Connect Zeno to your point-of-sale system via our REST API. Staff initiate a topup or charge from their POS terminal — the customer's Zeno wallet updates in real time. No separate hardware. No manual reconciliation. Every transaction is logged, hash-verified, and visible in both your dashboard and the customer's app.

REST API · Webhook Callbacks · Real-time Sync
# Initiate a wallet charge from your POS
POST /apikey/charge

{
  "walletId": "muw_38a7c...",
  "amount": 28.50,
  "orderId": "POS-20250503-0042",
  "note": "Table 7 dinner"
}

# Response — instant
{ "ok": true, "newBalance": 61.50, "expAwarded": 3 }

E-Commerce & Online Orders

Add Zeno wallet payment to your online checkout. Customers use their balance, earn EXP on orders, and see bonus credit apply automatically. Webhook callbacks confirm every payment.

Checkout Widget Available

Dining & Lifestyle Discovery

Zeno integrates with dining discovery and lifestyle platforms. Connect your customers' wallets to exclusive partner deals, vouchers, and cross-merchant offers in a single tap.

Partner Deal Marketplace

Booking & Reservation Systems

Connect your booking platform to Zeno's reservation API. Customers pay a preauth deposit directly from their Zeno wallet at booking — no credit card, no no-shows.

Preauth + Auto-release

Trigger Push Notifications via API

Fire targeted push notifications to your wallet customers directly from your own system — order confirmations, booking reminders, flash sale alerts. One API call reaches all.

Programmatic Push
