Enterprise-Grade E-Wallet Solutions for Growing Businesses

A single-location café has different needs from a restaurant group with 15 outlets. As your business grows, your loyalty and CRM tools need to scale with you — without scaling your costs.

What Enterprise-Grade Means

Enterprise-grade doesn't mean enterprise pricing. It means the platform can handle the complexity that comes with growth:

  • Multi-location management: One dashboard view across all your outlets
  • Centralised campaigns: Send push notifications to customers across all locations
  • Aggregated analytics: Compare performance between outlets
  • Consistent loyalty experience: Customers earn and spend across any of your locations
  • Staff access controls: Different permission levels for managers vs. staff

The Growth Challenge

Many loyalty platforms work great for one outlet but break down at scale. They either can't handle multiple locations, require expensive per-location licensing, or force you to run separate campaigns for each outlet. This creates operational headaches and inconsistent customer experiences.

Scaling with Zeno

Experience Zeno is built to grow with you. Whether you're a single café today or a 50-location chain tomorrow, the platform handles the complexity without changing your pricing (which is free) or requiring migration to a different tier.

Your customers don't care which of your locations they visit — they expect the same loyalty experience everywhere. Zeno delivers that seamlessly.
