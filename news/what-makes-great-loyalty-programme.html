What Makes a Great Loyalty Programme? Features That Actually Matter

With hundreds of loyalty programmes competing for your attention, how do you separate the great ones from the mediocre? Here are the features that actually matter — and the red flags to avoid.

1. Immediate Value

The best programmes reward you instantly. If you have to accumulate thousands of points over months before seeing any benefit, the programme is designed for the business, not the customer. Look for instant bonus credit or same-day rewards.

2. Simplicity

If you need to read a FAQ to understand how points convert to value, it's too complicated. Great loyalty programmes have simple mechanics: top up, get bonus. Spend, earn EXP. Reach threshold, unlock tier.

3. No Expiry

Point expiration is a deal-breaker. It penalises infrequent users and creates artificial urgency. The best programmes let your rewards persist indefinitely.

4. Multi-Merchant Coverage

A programme that works at one merchant is a stamp card with a screen. A programme that works across dozens of merchants is a genuine loyalty ecosystem.

5. Genuine Security

Your wallet should have biometric authentication, transaction alerts, and the ability to freeze instantly. If a programme stores your money, it must protect it seriously.

6. No Hidden Costs

Subscription fees, transaction charges, and withdrawal minimums all erode the value of loyalty rewards. The best programmes are completely free for consumers.
