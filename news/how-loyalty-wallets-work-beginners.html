How Loyalty Wallets Work: A Beginner's Guide to E-Wallet Rewards

If you've heard about loyalty wallets but aren't sure how they work, you're not alone. This guide breaks down the basics — what they are, how you earn, and why they're worth using.

What Is a Loyalty Wallet?

A loyalty wallet is a digital app that combines payment functionality with a rewards programme. Instead of just storing money like a regular e-wallet, a loyalty wallet actively rewards you every time you use it. Think of it as a wallet that pays you back.

How You Earn

With Experience Zeno, you earn in two ways:

Bonus Credit: When you top up money at a participating merchant, you receive extra credit instantly. Top up $50, get $53. The extra $3 is bonus credit — free money from the merchant.

EXP Points: Every topup also earns EXP (experience points). These accumulate over time and determine your loyalty tier. Higher tiers unlock better rewards.

Getting Started

  1. Download and sign up — free, takes under a minute
  2. Receive your welcome gift — free credit to try the system
  3. Find a participating merchant — look for Zeno merchants near you
  4. Top up — add money and watch your bonus credit appear
  5. Spend and earn — use your wallet at any Zeno merchant

Is It Safe?

Yes. Zeno uses PIN and biometric authentication, end-to-end encryption, and instant wallet freeze. Your money is protected by the same security standards used by banks.
