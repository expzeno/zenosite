FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

What is Experience Zeno?
Experience Zeno is a multi-merchant e-wallet and loyalty platform. You top up at participating merchants and receive instant bonus credit plus EXP points that grow your rewards over time.
How do I get bonus credit?
Every time you top up at a Zeno merchant, they add free bonus credit to your wallet instantly. The bonus rate varies by merchant — some give 10%, others more. It's real credit you can spend immediately.
What are EXP points?
EXP points are earned on every topup. As you accumulate EXP, you climb from Explorer to Voyager to Zenith — each tier unlocking higher earning rates and better rewards.
Is my money safe?
Yes. Every transaction is secured with PIN or biometric authentication, logged with SHA-256 hash verification, and your wallet can be frozen instantly if needed. Reservation deposits are held in escrow with full refund protection.
How do referrals work?
Share your referral link with friends. When they sign up and top up, you both earn bonus EXP. Your referral earning rate increases as you climb tiers.
Is it free to use?
Yes, the app is completely free. There are no subscription fees, no hidden charges. You simply top up and start earning.
Which merchants accept Zeno?
Zeno works with restaurants, cafés, salons, wellness centres, and other businesses across Singapore. Open the app to see the full list of participating merchants near you.
