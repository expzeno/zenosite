SECURITY & TRUST

Your money is protected

Bank-grade security at every layer — from encrypted transactions to biometric authentication.

⛓️
SHA-256 Hash Chain

Every transaction is chained to the previous one with a cryptographic hash — making any tampering instantly detectable.

📒
Double-Entry Bookkeeping

Every wallet movement has a matching ledger entry. Automated reconciliation runs continuously to verify every cent balances.

🔑
JWT RS256 Auth

2048-bit RSA key authentication with short-lived tokens, refresh rotation, and version-based revocation for stolen session protection.

🔒
Pessimistic Locking

Concurrent wallet operations use database-level row locking to prevent race conditions — your balance is always exactly right.

🛡️
AML Review

Large withdrawals trigger an automatic AML hold for admin review before funds are released — protecting merchants and customers.

👆
Biometric Authentication

Face ID, Touch ID, and device PIN protect your wallet session. All payments and session restores require biometric verification.

🔐
Wallet Lock Mode

Freeze your wallet instantly to block all new payments while keeping existing preauth reservations and topups fully active.

🏦
PDPA Compliant

Built to Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012. No payment card numbers or bank data stored — only what's needed to run the platform.

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

Is Experience Zeno free for customers?
Yes — there is no sign-up fee, no subscription, and no hidden charges. You only put money into a merchant wallet when you choose to top up.
What is bonus credit and how does it work?
Each merchant can configure a bonus rate on topups. When you top up (e.g. $50), the merchant's bonus is added to your wallet immediately (e.g. +$5 free credit). This is real spendable credit — not points you redeem later, not a voucher you print. It lands in your wallet the moment your topup is confirmed.
Can I use one wallet across multiple merchants?
Experience Zeno is a multi-merchant platform, but each wallet is isolated per merchant. You hold a separate wallet at each merchant — credit topped up at Merchant A can only be spent at Merchant A. This gives each merchant full control over their customer loyalty programme.
How do EXP points and loyalty tiers work?
You earn EXP automatically on every topup at any merchant. As your EXP balance grows, the platform awards you higher loyalty tiers (Explorer → Voyager → Zenith). Higher tiers unlock better referral earning rates — so the more you use Experience Zeno, the more you earn from your network.
How does the referral programme work?
Generate your unique referral code in the app. Share it with a friend — they sign up and top up their first wallet, earning a welcome EXP boost. From their second topup onward, you automatically earn referral EXP every time they top up at any merchant. Your referral earning rate increases as you advance tiers.
Can I withdraw my wallet balance?
Yes. You can request to withdraw your remaining wallet balance to a registered bank account. Withdrawals go through merchant approval and standard AML review before funds are released.
Is my money safe?
Every balance uses double-entry bookkeeping with a SHA-256 hash chain — making any tampering instantly detectable. All payments use database-level row locking to prevent race conditions. Experience Zeno is fully PDPA 2012 compliant and does not store payment card numbers or full bank account details.
