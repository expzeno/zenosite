FAQ

Real questions.
Straight answers.

No jargon. No runaround.

What is Experience Zeno?
Experience Zeno is Singapore's multi-merchant loyalty wallet. Top up at any partner merchant, get instant bonus credit added to your wallet, and earn EXP points that level you up over time. One app, all your merchants, more money every time.
How do I get bonus credit?
Every topup at a Zeno merchant triggers an automatic bonus credit from that merchant. They set their own rate — some give 10%, others more. It hits your wallet instantly. Real money, not rewards you redeem in 3 months.
What are EXP points?
EXP is your loyalty score. You earn it on every topup and referral. Accumulate enough to climb from Explorer to Voyager to Zenith — each tier unlocking higher rates and better benefits.
Is my money safe?
Yes. PIN or biometric locks every payment. SHA-256 hash logs every transaction. One-tap wallet freeze if your phone goes missing. Reservation deposits are held in escrow. Your money doesn't move unless you authorise it.
How do referrals work?
Share your code. When friends join and top up, you earn referral EXP every time they spend — automatically, from their second topup onward. Higher tiers earn more per referral.
Is it free to use?
100% free. No subscription. No transaction fee to the customer. No hidden anything. Top up, earn, and spend — that's it.
Which merchants accept Zeno?
Restaurants, cafés, hair salons, nail studios, wellness centres, bubble tea chains, and more — all across Singapore. Open the app to see the full live list near you.
